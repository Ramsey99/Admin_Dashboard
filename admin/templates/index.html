Frequently Asked Questions


How can I view the bus schedule?

You can view the bus schedule by navigating to the "View Bus Schedules" section in the app, where you can find bus timings, routes, and any updates on stoppages.

How do I book a bus ticket?

To book a bus ticket, go to the "Book Tickets" section in the app. Select your preferred route, date, and time, then confirm your booking by following the on-screen instructions.

How can I track the bus in real-time?

You can track the real-time location of the bus from the "Bus Location Tracking" feature. It shows you the live position of the bus on a map, along with the estimated arrival time at your stop.

Can I reschedule my bus ticket?

Yes, you can reschedule your bus ticket through the app. Visit the "My Bookings" section, select your booking, and follow the steps to change the date or time of your ticket.

How do I contact customer support for bus-related issues?

You can contact customer support through the app's contact form, or call our helpline at 123-456-7890 for immediate assistance.
